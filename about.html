About Me

Content Creator & Social Media Influencer

Hey there! I'm Topister Akinyi, a 19-year-old content creator from the beautiful luoland Homa Bay, Kenya. What started as sharing fun dance videos with friends has grown into a passion for creating content that celebrates Kenyan youth culture and brings joy to thousands of followers.

When I'm not filming TikTok dances or exploring new locations for my vlogs, you can find me at the beach with friends, discovering new music, or mentoring aspiring creators in my community.

My mission is to showcase authentic Kenyan experiences while proving that with creativity and consistency, anyone can turn their passion into a career in the digital space.

My Journey

2020

First TikTok Video

Posted my first dance video just for fun, not expecting the positive response it received from local viewers.

2021

Viral Breakthrough

My "Mombasa Sunset Challenge" video went viral with 500K+ views, gaining my first 10K followers.

2022

First Brand Partnership

Collaborated with Pwani Fashion Week on a sponsored campaign, marking my start as a professional creator.

2023

Full-Time Creator

Reached 100K+ followers across platforms and transitioned to content creation as my full-time career.

My Passions

Trend Creation

Developing original dance moves and challenges that showcase Kenyan culture while riding viral waves.

Storytelling

Capturing the vibrant energy of Mombasa's youth through authentic, engaging video content.

Community Building

Creating spaces online and offline where young creators can learn, collaborate and grow together.
